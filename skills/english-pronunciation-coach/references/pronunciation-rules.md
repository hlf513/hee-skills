### Primary Stress Mark

Use **ЋИ** before the stressed syllable in IPA: /…™mЋИp…ФЋРt…Щnt/

### Common Patterns

## Sentence Stress & Rhythm

### Content Words (Stressed)

These carry meaning вАФ typically stressed in a sentence:

- **Nouns**: dog, house, idea
- **Main verbs**: go, eat, think, decide
- **Adjectives**: big, happy, important
- **Adverbs**: quickly, very, often
- **Demonstratives**: this, that, these
- **Wh-question words**: what, where, when, why, how
- **Negative contractions**: don't, can't, won't

### Function Words (Unstressed / Weak)

These provide grammatical structure вАФ typically use weak forms:

- **Articles**: a, an, the
- **Pronouns**: he, she, it, they, me, us
- **Prepositions**: in, on, at, to, for, of, with
- **Conjunctions**: and, but, or, so
- **Auxiliary verbs**: is, am, are, do, have, will, can
- **Possessives**: my, your, his, her

### Stress for Contrast/New Information

Any word can be stressed for emphasis or contrast:

- "I **said** two, not three."
- "It was **her** idea, not his."
- "I **do** want to go." (contrastive stress on normally weak word)

### Rhythm Rule

English is **stress-timed**: stressed syllables occur at roughly equal intervals. Unstressed syllables are compressed between them.

## Intonation Patterns

### Falling Intonation вЖШ

Used for:
- **Statements**: I live in London. вЖШ
- **Wh-questions**: Where do you live? вЖШ
- **Commands**: Sit down. вЖШ
- **Exclamations**: How wonderful! вЖШ

### Rising Intonation вЖЧ

Used for:
- **Yes/No questions**: Do you like coffee? вЖЧ
- **Lists** (rise on each item, fall on last): I need вЖЧ milk, вЖЧ bread, вЖШ and eggs.
- **Incomplete thoughts**: I think that... вЖЧ
- **Uncertainty**: Really? вЖЧ

### Fall-Rise Intonation вЖШвЖЧ

Used for:
- **Implication/Correction**: "I think so вЖШвЖЧ" (but I'm not sure)
- **Contrast**: "Some people like it вЖШвЖЧ, but I don't."
- **Reservations**: "It's possible вЖШвЖЧ." (but unlikely)
- **Apologies**: "I'm sorry вЖШвЖЧ."

### Rise-Fall Intonation вЖЧвЖШ

Used for:
- **Strong agreement/disagreement**: "Absolutely! вЖЧвЖШ"
- **Surprise**: "You didn't! вЖЧвЖШ"
- **Certainty**: "Definitely. вЖЧвЖШ"
